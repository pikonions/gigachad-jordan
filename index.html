The city also has a thriving tourism industry, with many visitors coming to enjoy its beautiful beaches and natural attractions such as the Manggar Beach, Benua Patra Beach, and the Balikpapan Bay. my favorit food

nasi goyeng

Nasi goreng is a popular Indonesian fried rice dish that is often served as a main course. The name "nasi goreng" literally means "fried rice" in Indonesian. The dish is typically made by stir-frying pre-cooked rice with a mixture of vegetables, meat, and seasonings such as garlic, onion, soy sauce, and chili paste. Common additions to nasi goreng include chicken, shrimp, egg, and vegetables such as peas, carrots, and green onions. It is often garnished with slices of cucumber and tomato, and can be served with a variety of condiments such as sambal (a spicy chili paste) and kecap manis (a sweet soy sauce). Nasi goreng is a popular dish not only in Indonesia but also in many other Southeast Asian countries. It is known for its delicious taste, affordability, and versatility, and can be enjoyed as a quick street food snack or as a more elaborate meal in a restaurant setting.

bulu tangkis

Bulu tangkis, also known as badminton, is a popular racquet sport played by two or four players. It is played with a lightweight shuttlecock and a racquet, and the objective of the game is to hit the shuttlecock over the net and into the opponent's court in such a way that they are unable to return it. Bulu tangkis is believed to have originated in India in the 18th century, and it was introduced to England in the mid-19th century. The sport has since gained popularity around the world, particularly in countries such as Indonesia, China, and Malaysia, where it is considered a national sport. Bulu tangkis can be played as a singles or doubles game, and it requires a combination of skill, strategy, and physical endurance. Players must be able to move quickly and have good hand-eye coordination, as well as the ability to anticipate their opponent's moves and react accordingly. In addition to being a competitive sport, bulu tangkis can also be played for leisure and exercise. Many schools, community centers, and recreational facilities offer badminton courts for people of all ages and skill levels to enjoy.
